E.L. Laboratories, Inc. is the country's leading provider of progressive solutions to the industrial pharmaceutical manufacturing needs of leading Filipino and multinational pharmaceutical companies, and a trusted partner in specialty contract manufacturing.

We are a trusted contract manufacturing company founded on visionary Filipino leadership, highly qualified and committed pharmaceutical expertise, and advanced manufacturing innovations.

Our History

E.L. Laboratories, Inc. was established in 1996 to meet the growing contract manufacturing needs of Filipino and multinational pharmaceutical corporations under the leadership of Emerenciana Lingad-Bautista, who pioneered in the modern Philippine pharmaceutical industry after topping the Pharmaceutical Board Examination in 1950. She co-founded Associated Pharmaceuticals, Inc. in 1952, the first Filipino company to manufacture I.V. fluids in plastic pouches, and Elin Pharmaceuticals, Inc. in 1968, a top ranking Filipino pharmaceutical company.

Our Future

We believe that we are ready for a future of continued innovations to improve manufacturing flexibility, continued production of essential, high-value, low volume and complex pharmaceutical products, and of the continued provision of gainful employment to highly technical and qualified Filipinos.

We believe we're setting ourselves up for the future with the following strategies:
  • Strategic alliances with current clients to improve and expand product lines and develop new products.
  • Strategic alliances with other pharmaceutical companies to advance the interests of the Filipino pharmaceutical manufacturers.
  • Working closely with the FDA to have a common understanding of the implementation of GMP.
  • Working closely with academe in designing their curriculum to cater to the personnel requirements of the pharmaceutical manufacturing industry.
